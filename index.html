01 about

I'm a second-year Computer Science student at the University of Reading, heading into my final year with a specialisation in cybersecurity. My degree covers everything from machine learning and data visualisation to blockchain and computer security — but the thread running through my own projects is a hands-on, break-it-to-understand-it approach.

Outside coursework, I'm working through CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701), practising on TryHackMe, and building small tools that force me to actually implement concepts rather than just read about them — from a multithreaded port scanner to more involved detection and analysis projects.

I'm currently preparing applications for cybersecurity-focused postgraduate study and graduate schemes starting in autumn 2027.
